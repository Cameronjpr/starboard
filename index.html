HP: 100No cargo.Gold: 100


Use arrow keys to move.

Use your gold to buy resources from the islands on the map. Gain gold by selling those resources to islands that want them (marked by a target).

Avoid storms - they'll cost you both health (HP) and gold. If your health hits zero or you run out of money, you lose the game.

It's possible to buy something that no island wants. This is a known bug! In that case, press Q to dump your cargo.
